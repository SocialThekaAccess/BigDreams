Consultation Terms & Conditions

These terms outline how Big Dream handles consultation enquiries, appointment expectations, payments, rescheduling, and respectful conduct during meetings.

Bookings with trusted guidance

Purpose of a consultation

A consultation is intended to help you explain your current situation, discuss your migration or education goals, and receive general guidance on possible pathways, likely requirements, and next steps based on the information available at the time.

Accuracy of information provided

Advice given during a consultation depends on the facts and documents you provide. If material information is incomplete, inaccurate, or changes later, any guidance discussed may also change.

Booking and payment

Where a consultation fee applies, booking may require payment in advance or confirmation through our office. Any fee arrangements, inclusions, or documents requested before the appointment should be reviewed carefully before the consultation takes place.

Rescheduling and cancellations

If you need to reschedule or cancel, please contact Big Dream as early as possible. We aim to accommodate reasonable requests where scheduling allows, but repeated late changes or missed appointments may affect availability for future bookings.

Refunds and missed appointments

If a paid consultation is missed without notice, a refund may not be available. Where Big Dream needs to move or cancel an appointment due to unforeseen circumstances, we will aim to offer an alternative time or discuss the next available option.

Consultation scope

A consultation does not automatically create an ongoing service engagement. Further work such as document review, application preparation, representation, appeals support, or education placement assistance may require a separate agreement, additional instructions, and additional fees.

Respectful behaviour

We are committed to a respectful and safe environment. Harassment, intimidation, abusive language, discrimination, or threatening conduct toward staff may result in the consultation being ended or future service being declined.

Follow-up communication

After your consultation, Big Dream may contact you about next steps, requested documents, or relevant services connected to your enquiry. You can ask us not to send further follow-up communications at any time.

Contact details

Questions about these consultation terms can be directed to Big Dream Education Pty Ltd at admin@bigdreamvisa.com.au or +61 476 241 532.
