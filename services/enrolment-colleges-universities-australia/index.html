Frequently Asked Questions

Start Your Study Journey Today

Studying in Australia is a great way for students to gain a strong education, meet people from different countries, and build future career opportunities.

If you want to study in Australia, Big Dream Education and Visa Services can help you choose a course, apply to a college or university, and move through the process with confidence.

Q1. How do I apply to colleges and universities in Australia?

To apply, you need to choose a course, prepare your documents, submit your applications, and complete enrolment formalities.

Q2. What documents do I need for admission?

You will generally need academic documents, passport copies, English language test results, and additional supporting documents requested by the institution.

Q3. Can I work while studying in Australia?

Yes, international students can work while studying in Australia, but they must follow the conditions attached to their visa.

Q4. Which courses do students like?

International students often choose business, IT, healthcare, engineering, hospitality, and vocational programs.

Q5. Is IELTS required for studying in Australia?

Many institutions require English language test scores such as IELTS, PTE, or TOEFL. The requirement depends on the course and institution.

Q6. Do you help with student visas?

Yes, Big Dream Education and Visa Services helps with student visa applications and guides students through related procedures.
