What is Skilled Migration?

Skilled migration is a pathway for qualified workers who have skills that are needed in Australia. The Australian government regularly updates occupation lists based on industry demand and workforce shortages.

Applicants are usually assessed based on factors such as:

  • Age
  • Educational qualifications
  • Work experience
  • English language ability
  • Skills assessment
  • Occupation demand

Each profile is different, which is why understanding eligibility properly is important before starting the process.

Understanding the Skilled Migration Process

Many applicants feel confused because there are different stages involved in skilled migration. Understanding each step properly can help avoid unnecessary mistakes or delays.

Skills Assessment

Many jobs require a skills assessment from an authorised body to check whether your qualifications and work experience meet standards.

English Language Requirements

You may need tests like IELTS or PTE to prove your English skills and strengthen your application.

Expression of Interest (EOI)

An EOI shows your interest in migration and is ranked based on points and eligibility factors.

Visa Application

When you are eligible, you can move ahead with the visa process and submit the required documents for assessment.

Our team helps applicants at every stage so the visa application process becomes easier to understand and manage.

What is skilled migration?

Skilled migration is a pathway that allows qualified workers with in-demand skills to move to Australia. Occupation lists are regularly updated to reflect workforce shortages and industry demand.

Who can apply for migration?

People with the right occupation, qualifications, work experience, and English language ability may be able to apply, depending on the rules of the visa pathway.

Is IELTS required for migration?

Many skilled migration pathways require applicants to prove English language ability through tests such as IELTS or PTE.

What is a skills assessment?

A skills assessment checks whether an applicant's qualifications and work experience meet the standards required for a nominated occupation.

Can skilled migration lead to residency?

Some skilled migration pathways can lead to residency, depending on eligibility and the visa type involved.
