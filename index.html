FAQ

Common questions from prospective clients

Quick answers to some of the questions clients ask most often.

What services does Big Dream Education & Visa Services help with?

We support skills assessments, enrolments, student visas, skilled migration, employer sponsorship, partner and family visas, visitor visas, permanent pathways, graduate visas, and ART matters.

Can you help if I am unsure which visa pathway suits me?

Yes. An early eligibility review helps determine which pathway best fits your education, work experience, qualifications, and current circumstances.

Do you assist clients outside Perth?

Yes. Big Dream Education & Visa Services is based in Perth, Western Australia, but enquiry and advisory support can be provided remotely as well.

How do I get started?

Use the contact page to submit an enquiry, call directly, or book a consultation so your case can be assessed properly.
