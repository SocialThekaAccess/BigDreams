What services does Big Dream help with?

We support skills assessments, enrolments, student visas, skilled migration, employer sponsorship, partner and family visas, visitor visas, permanent pathways, graduate visas, and ART matters.

Can you help if I am unsure which visa pathway suits me?

Yes. An early eligibility review helps determine which pathway best fits your education, work experience, qualifications, and current circumstances.

Do you assist clients outside Perth?

Yes. Big Dream is based in Perth, Western Australia, but enquiry and advisory support can be provided remotely as well.
